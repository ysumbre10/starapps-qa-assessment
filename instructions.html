Before you begin

Read these rules carefully. The assessment starts the moment you click Begin Assessment.

Rules & Format

  1. 1
    7 tasks, one at a time. You will see one task at a time. The next task only appears after you submit the current one.
  2. 2
    10 minutes per domain. Each domain has 3 MCQs + 2 open tasks. One shared countdown covers all 5 items. When it reaches zero, everything is automatically submitted — even if incomplete.
  3. 3
    No going back. Once a task is submitted, it is locked. You cannot edit previous answers.
  4. 4
    Open-ended answers. There are no multiple-choice options. Type your answers in the provided text area. Code, lists, and plain prose are all fine.
  5. 5
    No collaboration tools. You may use documentation and your own knowledge. This is an individual assessment — please do not use AI assistants to generate your answers.
  6. 6
    You can submit early. If you finish a task before the 10 minutes are up, click Submit & Next Task to move on immediately.
  7. 7
    Stable connection required. Answers are saved when you submit each task. If your browser closes mid-task, that task's answer may be lost.

How answers are evaluated

There are no automated scores. Every response is reviewed by a human QA practitioner at Starapps Studio. We are looking for structured thinking, not keyword matching. A shorter, well-reasoned answer scores higher than a long, unfocused one.
